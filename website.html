What is Counseling Therapy?

Counseling therapy helps to understand their emotions , untold thought's, build self-awareness , and learn how to handle stress or life challenges .It is a safe space to talk about feelings, manage stress, improve relationships .

Types of Counseling

  • Individual Therapy
  • Career Counseling
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Benefits of Therapy

Counseling therapy offers many benefits:

  • Improved mental health
  • Better communication skills
  • Stress and anxiety management
  • Healing from trauma
  • Personal growth and self-awareness
